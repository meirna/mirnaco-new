EUSA Combat Championships 2019

Aftermovie

Video recap of an international sports competition held in Zagreb.

European Universities Combat Championships was the biggest event hosted by the European Universities Sports Association (EUSA) in 2019.

1300 participants from 404 European universities and 36 countries competed in karate, judo, taekwondo and kickboxing at four locations in Zagreb, Croatia. Coordinated by the Croatian Academic Sports Federation.

My roles: camera, editing/post-production.

Making of 'Invocation' by Šimun Matišić Sextet

Promo video

Scenes from an 8-hour live recording session of a jazz album.

Six outstanding jazz musicians got together at HRT Zvonimir Bajsić studio to record an album written by 20-year old vibraphonist Šimun Matišić. Eight complex yet beautifully melodic compositions were recorded live in a total of 8 hours, making it a joy to witness and document.

The album was recorded and mixed by Luka Grubišić-Čabo at SoundBrick Studio in Zagreb, and mastered at Abbey Road.

My roles: camera and AV production assistant.

(What's Wrong with) Record Store Day

Short documentary

Short documentary addressing some of the criticism Record Store Day has received in recent years.

A friend and I attended several Record Store Day events in Glasgow. We spoke to visitors and organizers of the various events.

Among others, we interviewed Alex Main, owner of a well known Glaswegian independent record shop/non-profit organisation New Hellfire Club. Another interviee is Hector Barbour (a.k.a. techno producer Denis Sulta), staff member of Rubadub, a store that has famously declared its own Rubadub Shop Day in place of Record Store Day.

My roles: camera, script, voiceover, editing/post-production.
